HI FRIEND!
I'M KATELIN –

SAHM sharing low-tox swaps, girl mom finds, and balanced living with love.

Hi friend! So glad you're here. I started a health journey after college and ended up down the rabbit hole of "clean" foods after working with a functional medicine doctor. Then slowly over the years I got a little more into my low-tox journey. Like many others, once I had a baby I started looking into baby products and it infiltrated into my entire house.

I became passionate about finding ways to swap to nontoxic products that were affordable, and not consume my life with anxiety. Balance was very important to me! I hope I can help you find balance and easy swaps in your home. Check out my blog for lists of topics and swaps.

Remember, progress over perfection - every small swap counts!
