Signs Your Baby is Teething

Before diving into remedies, here are the telltale signs we noticed:

  • Excessive drooling (like, soaking through bibs)
  • Wanting to chew on everything
  • Disrupted sleep patterns
  • Increased fussiness, especially at night
  • Red, swollen, or tender gums
  • Changes in eating habits

Natural Remedies That Actually Worked

The Science Behind Natural Teething Relief

Many natural remedies work by providing gentle numbing effects or anti-inflammatory properties. Homeopathic remedies like those in Camilia are designed to work with your baby's natural healing processes.

Frozen Washcloths & Teething Toys

The simplest solutions are often the best. We kept clean washcloths in the freezer and rotated teething toys through the fridge.

Pros:
  • Completely natural and safe
  • Provides immediate relief
  • No cost involved
  • Easy to clean and reuse
Cons:
  • Temporary relief only
  • Need to constantly rotate items
  • Can be messy with all the drool

Breast Milk Popsicles

Game changer! I froze breast milk in ice cube trays and let her gnaw on them in a mesh feeder.

Pros:
  • Nutritious and familiar taste
  • Cold provides numbing relief
  • Uses excess pumped milk
  • Baby loves the taste
Cons:
  • Only works if breastfeeding
  • Can be messy
  • Need mesh feeders for safety
